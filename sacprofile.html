About the Student Activity Center

It is a most lively place on the campus providing a safe, comfortable and friendly environment that enables learning and advancement of knowledge, and promotes discovery and scholarship. The mission of the Central Library is to facilitate creation of new knowledge through acquisition, organization and dissemination of knowledge resources and providing for value added services.

No Dues

About the No Due Process

During your curriculam the institute encourages students to participate and explore various academic and extracarricular facilities to enhance your learning experience throughout your time in the MNNIT campus.

This section is crucial to help students clear out any pending dues they may have towards the library before leaving the institution. You can view the list of students who apply for fresh no dues in the "New Requests" section. The students who have cleared their dues towards the library and have been approved can be viewed in the "Approved Requests" section. The students who have not yet cleared their dues and have been put on hold for approval can be viewed in the "Rejected Requests" section. Kindly provide an accurate reason to reject any no dues request, as it helps keep an accurate record of student activity.
