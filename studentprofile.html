No Dues

About No Dues Process

During your curriculam the institute encourages you to participate and explore various academic and extracarricular facilities to enhance your learning experience throughout your time in the MNNIT campus. Therefore it is importent to clear out any pending dues you may have towards these departments before you leave the institution. These Departments are : Library, Deputy Registarar(Accounts), Concerning Hostel Warden and Chief Warden, Dean(Student Welfare)/Chief Proctor, Student Activity Centre(SAC)/Gymkhana, Alumni Association and Concering Head of Department.

You can apply for no dues here at the time of leaving the institute, Track the progress of your application and Print out the application form once it has been approved by all the concering deparments to finally be approved by Dean(Academics)
