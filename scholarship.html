Scholarship and Fellowship

About Scholarships and Fellowships

Students are required to follow the guidelines of the scholarship providing agency and apply for scholarship/stipend/aid accordingly. Further all students who are applying for any scholarship are required to fill relevant undertaking (Form: DSW-03/Form: DSW -04)

Scholarship Stipend/Aid : Stipend to stipendiary Ph.D. Students.

Insuarance and Mediclaim

About Insurance and Mediclaim

Institute has engaged National Insurance Company Limited (A Government of India undertaking) for session 2018-19 to cover bonafide students of the Institute for hospitalization against illness and accidental injury subject to policy conditions as given below. To avail this facility, a student shall first approach the Health Centre of the Institute and Doctor available at the Health Centre may refer the student to the listed Hospital, if required. In case of emergency [during night or Institute holidays], student may directly approach the listed hospital.

Policy Features

  1. 01

    Maximum extent of cover against hospitalization due to illness or accidental injury is Rs. 50,000 per year.

  2. 02

    The mediclaim benefit available to the students is cashless at designated hospitals and through TPA, if treatment is being taken at non designated hospitals then claim will be reimbursed to the student on receipt of complete claim documents through TPA.

  3. 03

    The policy covers permanent total disability due to accident and accidental death coverage of the insured student arising out of any accident during the coverage period as per coverage chart / policy.

  4. 04

    The policy covers accidental death and permanent total disablement for earning parents of students arising out of any accident during the coverage period as per coverage chart / policy.

  5. 05

    Treatments under Allopathic System of Medication are only covered.

  6. 06

    Dental treatments & Physiotherapy are not covered for claims / reimbursements.

  7. 07

    Hospitalization for minimum period of 24 Hours is mandatory.

  8. 08

    Death, permanent total disablement and injury due to accident are only covered.

  9. 09

    Death due to mosquito bite / insect bite are excluded.

  10. 10

    Intimation for hospitalization should be given within 24 hrs to Office of the Dean (SW).

  11. 11

    Theft of Pedal Cycle from Institute is covered only when it is in the name of student and intimation to police is given. It is permissible once in a year. Intimation of Bi-cycle theft should be given within 24 hrs to Office of the Dean (SW).

  12. 12

    Reimbursement of tuition / examination fee will be subject to hospitalization of students after accident and satisfactory investigation.

  13. 13

    Student ID card issued by institute will be considered for cashless claim facility.

  14. 14

    For detailed Terms, conditions and exclusions please refer on Page 5/18 onwards.

Anti-Ragging Initiative

About Anti-Ragging Policy

Institute has zero tolerance against any kind of ragging. Students are strictly advised not to indulge in any kind of activity which comes under ragging. “Don’t rag and also don’t allow ragging others” is the message given to the students.
The Anti-ragging Committee supervises the activities in the Institute campus to make the campus ragging free. Regular meeting of the Committee is convened to get the updates and remedial actions are taken to make the environment of the Institute congenial to every student.

Counseling and Assistance

About Counseling and Assistance

A sub-section “Faculty in charge (Students counseling Boys) and (Students counseling Girls)” is created under the office of Dean (SW) to provide common platform where the students can interact friendly and frankly with his/her counselor regarding any academic or non-academic (personal if a student want to share) issues of their concern. The role of student counselor is like a guide who listen their problems properly and provide the solutions based on his/her capability or provide a road map to solve their issues in due course of time and build self-confidence among them for their personal growth and positive change through self-understanding. Counselors visit regularly to respective hostels to interact with the students to know their problem(s), if any. Students may also directly contact the counselor(s) assigned for respective hostel for interaction and discussion about any personal or academic problem being faced by him/her. All possible efforts are made for solving the problem of the student, without disclosing the identity, by the counselors to their best. Number of counseling sessions is also organized for the students where an expert interacts with the students so that students may lead to a successful and stress free campus life.
